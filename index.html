About Me

Hi there! I'm a Front-End Developer who enjoys building clean and modern websites. As a developer, I aim to create the perfect union between engineering and design for an effective and enjoyable user experience.

I've been fortunate enough to gain a wide range of skills from both personal projects and professional experience. From API creation to feature implementation and everything in between. It's my belief that, at the end of the day—Good solutions are a win for everybody!

My Philosophy

As a professional, I value knowledge, creativity, and adaptability. I see the world through a realistic and logical lens. Because of this, I try to lead with what I do know and stay humble about what I do not know.

With an A.S. in Digital Media and Multimedia, I have experience in both web design and development which helps me understand the bigger picture. I take into account the aesthetic quality of a website, as well as the underlying performance of the codebase.

When working with clients and my team, I seek to foster a positive and collaborative environment where a project's goals can be understood and met. Simply put, I value respect and empathy in any workplace I am involved with.

The Person Behind The Screen

I'm sure you won't be surprised to find out that I am indeed a normal person outside of my career. In truth, my boundless curiosity often drives me to pursue a wide range of passions.

Typically, you will find me involved with something creative like writing or music composition. I also enjoy cooking, reading a novel, studying a new subject, and even modding games and software to get them just right
